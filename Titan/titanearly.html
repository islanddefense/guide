Grace period

Before you even spawn in it's good to consider a few things so you can focus on instantly doing productive things. Things to consider are:

  • Are my settings correct? bans, camera, vb, oldkeys, silence, inventory hotkeys
  • What titan am I playing?
    • What are my starting items with this titan?
    • If it's random what would my starting items be? If I random a particular titan, is there an exception to what I'm buying?
    • What is my skill build with this titan? Do I hold a skill point incase I encounter an eb?
  • Who are the builders?
    • Do some of the builders lumber base at the same area every game?
    • Where am I pearling?
      • Should I pearl before I buy my items incase I can exploit any early base?
    • Where and when am I using my scout?
    • If my pearl and my scout show me nothing, where would be the best direction to explore?
    • What builder is easily abuseable for early feed?
      • If you have the option to attack someone who played 10k games vs 1k games it's more likely the 1k game player will fail to deto fruits etc.
  • How do I plan on getting a minion this game?
    • This goes hand in hand with your item build.
    • If you don't mix up your methods of getting minions you will become very predictable and might struggle after a few games once builders are aware of the trick.

Early game principles

Every titan early game will be different depending on what information you have and in what ways builders expose feed, but any early game should follow these principles. How you play in the early game will determine much of the rest of the game so it's important to get it right.

  • Kill every unupgraded wall you see.
  • Never show builders when you enter invis or break invis, unless breaking will yield a minion.
  • Frequently put your camera at mid the first 2 minutes to see which direction builders come from, their lumber base is most likely in that direction.
  • Put ankh on mound inventory, give it to your minion once you get one.
  • Spend your gold as you get it, no need to jork with 300g waiting to buy a full item, buy the small components.
  • You need at least 3x tier2 damage items to effectively kill upgraded walls, for example 2x berserker gems + titanic trident
  • Use your lumber, cyclone is good even if builders have workers mid.
  • If you see a lazy radioactive or nature seeding lazily in one area, it's worth to spend some time nuking there, in previous versions it wasn't but in the current version you basically have infinite mana.
  • Tidals spawns are great, they cost 250g and if you clear one full det 6x fruit 1x RC, you've made your gold back after selling it later.
  • Gnoll and makrura are free kills with wotw and cyclone
  • Morphling is vulnerable to ward blocks
  • If builders have lots of hunters you must buy runic axe and farm them
  • Webbed feet are a nice item, but not for running around the map in fog, if you are in the fog builders can't gold and it doesn't matter if you run 20% faster if when if you do decide to show and kill walls 20% slower.
  • Only show on the map when you want to, avoid accidentally entering vision, for example if you see a hunter which will obviously blink across some unchasable path, don't show and attack it (unless you have runic).
  • In many cases you will be minionless at level 5, in which case you want level 3 nuke, if we don't spend the ability point at level 4 we can use it to skill level 3 nuke + unique at level 5.
  • Once you have enough damage items you can easily control mid and clear it off from all walls, at this point you are staying permanently mid until builders stop walling, this forces builders to stay far from mound giving you a big window of opportunity where you can show on the map while builders are spending ~15 seconds to run to gold, reducing their gold income drastically.

Getting a minion or exploiting builder laziness

Whatever method you comitt to, for example tidal, mirror, mana mound. Know the first attempt will be the attempt most likely to succeed, once builders know you committed gold to a method they will prepare and take measures against it. Practice a play in singleplayer, not multiplayer.

Item jump

Item jumping into any area where builder don't expect you is usually a good idea. Go singplayer a practice jumping and picking up the item quickly so builders don't see where the jump happened, builders can still hear the item drop from quite far though.

Lazy full dets

A non-lazy full det needs to be immune to item jumping, mirror jumping, tidal spawns, ward jumps, teleporting to exp glyphs. If you identify a full deto lumber base which is exploitable, consider doing so. Usually there are just a few builder players able to micro workers mid to dispel cyclones, if you can remove their full dets you remove workers mid and you can cyclone builders freely.

Fog tp

Have a look at the teleport animation. See how on the titan the animation is just shown the first 1.5 seconds and then 2 seconds of no animation. We can abuse this if our mound is in fog, by teleporting while titan is in fog followed after the animation ends by attacking a wall at the edges of the map, and then WWing once we are teleported. The builders point of view will be:

  1. "Your allies are under attack" notifaction.
  2. Click space to see whats happening.
  3. See titan at the edge of the map and go gold.

This is why it's so important for builders to always have vision of mound, to block this play.

Titan can do a similar play in the early game while builders are still running mid. Before builders are running mid they are inside their walls, waiting for titan to show on the map, if we attack one side of the map builders will assume we remain in this area and builders on the opposite side of the map will feel safe, so we tp in fog and head the other direction where they are safe and ww and kill. However this all requires the mound to be in fog and the first minute after spawning is daytime so it only works if we can do it very early before the supports make it mid.

Real fake tp

Using a tp staff on mound will not display a status bar effect, making builders think it's a fake tp. To make this play work you need to tp in fog, but you must have recently showed on the edges of the map so builders feel safe. It's good to use the fake tp ability a couple times to condition the builders into a false sense of security. You will always spawn 5'o clock using a tp staff (assuming nothing is blocking)

"Allied base is under attack bait"

Similar to fog tp we can exploit builders who don't click space before golding. If use a unit such as fire spirit (spawned from fog) or tidal spawn we can force an "Allied base is under attack" notification, builders think it's titan and exit walls where we can wait in invis. Ward jump

While ward jumping is largely obsolote due to item jumping being superior, there are geometries which you can't item jump but can ward jump.

Ward blocks

The most reliable method to kill good players is with ward blocks, if you can get an autoattack plus nuke a 300hp builder is usually dead with enough damage items.

Neutralizing wand

Neut wand removes basically all buffs for 100g, it's incredibly powerful and builders don't expect their panic to be dispelled. It one shots any summoned unit.

Tier 3 nuke rod / tidal scepter

This is a desperation buy, if you don't have a minion and 850g to spare you can buy tidal scepter and it will together with your level 3 nuke one shot a 300hp builder. It is worth to buy for the minion but it will set your economy back 100 - 0.5*850 = -325g in a game you are already most likely struggling in.

Mana mound / wotws / cyclones

You can chain up to 3 cyclones or even more with using wotw from mound, cyclone ability, and wotw from titan, if there are low amount of workers mid or you expect whoever is controlling them is slow it could be a good idea. Even if the dispel is quick, it could yield a minion by delaying builders movement by just a few fractions of a second.

Pheonix jumping

This is how you style on noobs. As with ward jumps there is little benefit to jumping like this, but there are instances something could be jumpable with a phoenix egg or wards but not with items. Check this video to see how it's done, the video was recorded before item jump was implemented. It's preferable to jump with phoenix than with voodoo doll from mound because of the gold cost of course.
