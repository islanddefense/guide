Shelter mechanics

In certain tree geometries when you have 2 shelters too close to each other, harvesters can get confused to which shelter they return lumber to and become stuck. The rule to avoid this is to build your shelters from bottom to top, or from left to right.

Check video for reference, first example is correct and second is incorrect and a harvester becomes stuck.

This is obscure knowledge from gogblitz of the vampirism community. It is important to know about this mechanic if you are ever basing in hermit or stronghold for example, it's a good habit to always try to follow this rule as in my experience workers misbehave a lot when they become collisionless from returning lumber. If you don't follow this rule your lumber income will be reduced as the bugged worker will remain idle until you force it to return lumber and for faerie it's even more crucial as her workers don't have an animation you will never notice that they are idle.
