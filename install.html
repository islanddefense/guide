Installing Warcraft

As of November 2025 original Warcraft III cd keys are unable to be linked to your battlenet account, if you don't own Warcraft III on your battlenet account you will need to buy a copy at https://playwarcraft3.com/.

Purchase and install warcraft 3 using the battlenet launcher. Standard edition is fine and what most people use, the spoils of war edition is not required to play Island Defense.

Playing Island Defense

  1. Open battlenet launcher
  2. Select Warcraft III: Reforged
  3. Click the blue play button.
  4. Click Multiplayer
  5. Click custom games
  6. In the search bar, type: island
  7. Join an island defense lobby
    • If no lobby appears either host one yourself your wait for someone to host.
    • There is no port forwarding required to host in 2026 as it was before, anyone can host and the game will be hosted on a blizzard server.

Once lobbies start going they will fill quickly, you need to watch the discord to see the progress of games so you don't miss it.
