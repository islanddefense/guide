### Spawning in

After you spawn in you need to quickly create a temporary lumber at mid. If you picked and started with 30 lumber you can return lumber from your workers quickly for another 20 lumber. Prepare your fruits to be killed by ordering them to attack each other until they are one attack from dying. Once the grace period is at 13 seconds order the fruits to attack the shelter and queue attack orders on both of the fruits. Detonate the workers when titan spawns, this should be lined up with when the shelter dies. 

### Setting up an unabusable full det

Next up create a full det lumber base. It's important you protect your lumber base vs item jump, tidal spawns, and ethereal mirror. See item jump section to prevent item jump. For tidal spawns you have a couple of choices, either you block 1x1 openings with 99% built buildings so you can cancel them if someone wants to pass through, or you can have your fruits deep in the lumber base so the tidal needs to walk in vision giving you time to deto your fruits for example if you lumber in LB1 (above bay) you can put your fruits in bay. For mirror you need to have two layers of walls separated by around 600 range so if titan mirrors one layer, you will have another layer giving you time to det.

### Golding

Never gold unless there exists workers mid to dispel any cyclone, exception to this are builders who are extremely hard to kill like demonologist, radioactive, or draenei.

### Good principles to follow

If you build a wall you need to upgrade it, if titan doesn't have minions and it's sub 10 minutes into the game. Unupgraded walls doesn't force the titan to show on the map for any meaningful time giving builders time to gold. If you upgrade them titan is giving builders gold every time he chooses to attack one. 

Try to build item jump proofed walls around mid, some mid entrances require you to build an extra wall for this, except the north east entrance.

Ideally you always want vision of gold mound so you always see if titan spawns a spirit of fire or teleports in from fog, using speed turtles or workers are suitable for this.

Check the titan section to see any tricks and make sure you are not vulnerable.
