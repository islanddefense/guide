# Wallwalk

Orc style builders are immune to damage while building, which means we can chain build buildings to dodge any damage. 

This requires high apm and a good keyboard, your <kbd>esc</kbd> key must be close to your <kbd>1</kbd> key. If your escape key is in narnia like for me, you need to buy a new keyboard or rebind <kbd>esc</kbd> to a key which neighbours <kbd>1</kbd>. I rebound <kbd>§</kbd> (key left of 1 on swedish keyboards) to <kbd>esc</kbd>

**Click process:**

- <kbd>b</kbd> open build menu.
- <kbd>w</kbd> select wall, or fruit, or rc, or any building
- <kbd>lclick</kbd> build it
    - depending on your ping, let's say you have 100ms, you can 99ms before your builder enters the wall "queue" up the next action, either a move action or building action. I say queue with quotes because I don't mean you should shift queue the action, if you just do an action 99ms before entering the wall the action you order will come to the game server 1ms after you enter the building. 
- <kbd>b</kbd><kbd>w</kbd> for another wall and <kbd>lclick</kbd> to build it or issue a move command
- <kbd>lclick hold</kbd> box select the wall
- <kbd>esc</kbd> to cancel the building
- <kbd>1</kbd> select your builder
- queue up the next action and repeat the process.
- I move my camera with middlemouse click, this is key to wallwalking over a large area, you need your camera centered on the action but you can't move your mouse too far from where you are building if you edge pan. 


**Hand motion:**

This is different for everyone, do whatever works for you. I have my index finger on <kbd>b</kbd> and pinky on <kbd>w</kbd> and then I move my hand in a swoop to click <kbd>esc</kbd> with my ring finger and <kbd>1</kbd> with my middle finger, and alternate back and forth like this.

Video showing how it's done. Video starts at normal speed and then slows down to 25% and 10% speed. When I click <kbd>§</kbd> the software I'm using thinks it's a <kbd>|</kbd> or <kbd>\</kbd> button, and as I said it's rebound with a simple ahk script to output <kbd>esc</kbd> if clicked.

??? tip "Autohotkey / script disclaimer"
    Any macro, script, or other tool which makes two or more actions into one is cheating. Some people with questionable morals prefer to take shortcuts and automate away as much as possible to adjust for their poor skill. For example instantly detonating harvesters or automating any sequence in wallwalking. In 2026 there are no hostbots and central authority which ensures our games are free of cheaters. Instead if we encounter cheating in our lobbies we must save the evidence and share it so we can, hopefully, collectively keep that player banned from our lobbies if evidence is sufficient.
