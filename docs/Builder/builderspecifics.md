### Hunters

Troll, gnoll, dryad, pirate, goblin, arachnid should level their hunters and have it defend their base.

### Demonologist

Seeding is feeding, make a full det. You are forced to deto your workers after one level 2 nuke, forcing you to recreate pools and possibly feed them, but also while you are waiting for a good time to rebuild pools you are without lumber income. Your shelter feeds a lot if titan were to buy tidals, and it will feed after minions and miniminions come out, your RC is also easy feed since if you are seeding you most likely put the RC randomly out in the open. 

You will arrive at mid later though if you full det, which might have an impact versus misermaxxing camp titans, in those games it might be fine to seed and then once the camping begins you transfer your seed to a full det.

### Goblin

Seeding is feeding, early basing is feeding, make a full det. It's also worth to make a lumber base at mid after spawning just like all other builders.

### Magnataur

Very susceptible to die early, spend your first 5g to buy a wotw in the shop, any builder who buys it before mag can get it is griefing.
