# Control groups

You can keybind any selection of your own or shared allied units by clicking <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>0</kbd>-<kbd>9</kbd>. This will bind that selection to that number. You can also use <kbd>Shift</kbd>, this will instead add to the group, compared to <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> which will completely override whichever selection that number currently had. You should use both methods depending on what you need, for example keybinding your towers later it's nice to use <kbd>Shift</kbd> to add to your selection as you build towers.

I'm not an expert on micro and what the optimal control group organization is, play with whichever groups you prefer, but here are mine. 

**[Builder]**

**Early game**

- <kbd>1</kbd> Builder
- <kbd>2</kbd> Shelter in lumber base
- <kbd>3</kbd> Research center
- <kbd>4</kbd> Initially control grouped shelter at mid which produces workers to dispel cyclones, I then shift add the units it produces to add to the control group. The result is whenever the workers die my initial shelter is still bound to this key.
- <kbd>5</kbd> Shelter which produces workers to gather lumber when I don't need workers dispelling at mound

**Late game**

- <kbd>1</kbd> Builder
- <kbd>2</kbd> Ulti towers
- <kbd>3</kbd> Worker block shelters
- <kbd>4</kbd> - <kbd>9</kbd> Towers

**[Titan]**

- <kbd>1</kbd> Titan
- <kbd>2</kbd> Mound + research center
- <kbd>3</kbd> Summons such as phoenix, spirit of fire, tidal
    - Later I will put all my minions on this
- <kbd>4</kbd> <kbd>5</kbd> <kbd>6</kbd> Minions in pairs of 2
