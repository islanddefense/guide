### Recommended Warcraft III Settings

With reforged many new settings are unlocked, most are visual and have no effect on gameplay, do whatever you wish with these settings. I'll list some settings I recommend below:

- **Video:**
    - Window Mode: Windowed Fullscreen
- **Sound:**
    - 3D Positional Audio: enabled
- **Gameplay:**
    - Show Unit Life Bars: Damaged or always, i play with damaged
    - Team Colored Life Bars: Disabled
    - Use Special Hero Status Bars: Enabled
    - Show Hero Level: Enabled
    - Enhanced Tooltips: Enabled
    - Subgroup Order Modifier Key: Enabled
    - Enable Formation Movement Toggle: Disable
    - Show Number For Cooldowns: Enable
    - Show Deny Icon: Enable
    - Hud Side Panels: Disable
    - Background: TFT Classic (for max nostalgia)
    - Enabled Improved Zoom: Preferential, island defense camera system is sufficient for a nice camera distance, I play with this disabled but you can enable it.
    - Fade Occluding Objects: Enabled, trees become transparent if they are blocking the view of units.
    - Automatically Save Replays: Enable
- **Input:**
    - Keybinding: Classic, island defense already has gridlike hotkeys which are ergonomical. 

### In Game Commands
- /fps Displays your FPS
- /ping Displays your ping
- /apm displays your APM
- /time displays the ingame elapsed time

### Host Commands

- !openall

### Island Defense Commands

- -cam x (-cam 135)
    - Most players make the mistake of playing too zoomed out with cams above 170, to each their own what they prefer and it depends on the size of your monitor. I play with 135 because that's what the best player used when I started playing, I think it's necessary to accurately and quickly click in the right spots to execute jumps/blocks. 
- -cam lock
- -cam scroll
- -ban builder1 builder2 builder3 builder4 
- -ban titan1 titan2 titan3
- -silence (mutes builder/titan produced voice lines)
- -vb [on|off] (toggles vote board)
- -ts [on|off] (toggles titan shop UI)
- -vk [playername|color] (votes to kick player)
- -boot [playername|color] (votes to kick observer)
- -sh [on|off] (toggles smart heal for titan)
- -kick [playername|color] (for titan to boot a minion to observers)
- -oldkeys (swaps the dispel hotkey in gold mound from the default <kbd>Q</kbd> to the old bind <kbd>S</kbd>)
