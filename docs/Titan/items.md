### Thoughts on items

I don't want to give a general item build, most builds can work as long as you play to the items idea, don't buy t2 pearl + webbed feet and try to farm upgraded walls for mid for example if that was the gameplay you want to go for then you should've bought damage. As I've tried to convey so far how you can exploit builder laziness/weaknesses will vary between games, and if you repeat yourself too much builders will be less likely to fall for it. I gave a general noob build in the first time titan section for those insisting they want a build to follow.

Instead I'll share my thoughts on some items.

- **t1 pearl** okay nice item we get for free, in case we are poor and need to damage maxx it's okay to put it on mound and buy a full 6 slot of inventory of dmg items
- **t2 pearl** necessary to buy after your first minion when builders are basing
- **t3 pearl / msog** broken item if you can afford it once you got a minion
- **gem of haste** best damage item when you are poor
- **berserkers gem** great damage item which goes well with any other damage item
- **titanic trident** great damage item
- **voodoo doll** great item for damage, blocking, and jumping
- **armor of tides** okay defensive item
- **dawnbreaker** good defensive item
- **t3 essence** great defensive item to reset during a siege
- **runic axe** okay item, necessary to buy if builders have hunters
- **stormrider** nice utility for sieging or threatening builders early
- **ethereal mirror** underbought item, very good on molt immolation works while ethereal
- **robe of spellcraft** great item as well 10 seconds to regen to full, molt immolation works in it
- **casque of valor** great if you are rich you can give to a minion
- **ankh of superiority** nice item but ankh should be on your minion so not that useful
- **magic armor** necessary vs radioactive gold base, gnoll, or faerie base
- **wand of neutralization** insanely strong to negate any builder buff
- **tome of retraining** if you want to siege and your heal isn't max level buy this to retrain, can have level 4 heal at level 7.
- **scroll of the beast** give to minions to boost your damage when you siege
