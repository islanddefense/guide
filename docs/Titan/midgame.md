### Midgame

The midgame starts whenever you get a minion and builders are forced to base. If you haven't bought it already you need to buy t2 pearl and webbed feet at this point, because you need information and you want good efficiency moving around the map.

Try to figure out where the builders like to base, some players keep basing over and over in the same base. Builders are usually not bad and will never base when you are near. If you want to bait out a hermit base for example you can show your titan on the right side of the map and builders will base on the left. If you are quick to pearl (out of vision) and tp back mid you can catch them while basing.

Identify which builders are high lumber and try to kill their lumber bases. If you suspect lumber pooling you must pearl the secret shop. I see many good titans forget about pooling and they lose free win games because builders can pool freely.

If you see a builder who has been spamming upgraded walls in the early game they will not be able to base, if you see them basing in the midgame pearl their base and confirm that their towers are low tier and siege quickly. You can also fraud check solo bases early in the mid game, for example if somoene is basing at 10 minutes with 1k lumber per minute, they usually spend 6k lumber on walls, arc, shelters, fruits, rc, lumber acquisition, worker health. Meaning they have 4k left to spend on towers, which is enough for 20 enhanced towers, which any modestly farmed titan should be able to break with a minion at 10 minutes, you need at least eternal wards and t2 armor. 

### How to siege

Ideally for any siege you want druid leaf and a floating eye. The floater is to prevent rewalling, in the case you can't  prevent it, for example if you don't have enough minions or if the rewaller is unkillable the floater loses value, but it's still good so you can shift queue actions. The druid leaf to reset outside the base and healing during the siege, in past version you would be somewhat fine to just have eternal wards and maybe an unupgraded ward for a budget build, but this version it gives inherit regen so it has insane value and is always worth to buy. 
You need to hold position with you minions and titan when not moving, otherwise they will try to path to kill towers which is impossible and take unecessary damage.
The key to good micro when sieging is to shift queue actions on your titan while you manually control minions to place wards, heals, nukes. Worker block complicates this, you can't shift queue walls when there are workers blocking your path. To deal with worker block you should be nuking them but also hold position so you will as per the attack priority attack workers.

You can skill check whoever you are sieging by drawing aggro from a few towers on each minion either by walking them in first or by going ww mid siege for a second. 

You can abuse resetting while sieging and never fully commit, for example teleporting out, using essence for invulnerability (and basically full heal), ww, mistlords, become ethereal. If you have enough minions to prevent rewalling you can resett infinitely until they are forced to rewall and die to nuke.

### Cheesing unwinnable games

If you for whatever reason had a bad early and mid game and somehow didn't get rushed, you can try to cheese a win. Either by ankh jumping (high skill), mirror jumping (low skill), baiting a 300hp builder to rewall and you are ready with nuke + t3 nukerod, bridge a chain nuke with an albatross (you can influence albatross movement by using an eye of the ocean on them), or by fog nuking which is to with floating eyes create a path where your nuke will travel to the builder instead of nearby workers which are in the fog.

As an ethical titan player myself my rule to cheesing is to only do it versus players who should know better than to get caught by cheese strats. Mirror jumping someone who barely has played the map is a great way to get them to never play again.
