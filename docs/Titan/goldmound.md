### Gold mound and its abilities
You need to control group mound and the research center. You spend lumber for these upgrades.

- Dispel (free)
    - Must use this to instantly clear any debuff you have
- Teleport (free)
- Fake teleport (free)
- Fire spawn
    - Great value
- Chimera courier
- Gravity well
- Cyclone
    - Great value
- Titan armor upgrade
    - Spend your lumber on this after you got a minion
- Titan damage upgrade
- Titan health upgrade
- Inventory
    - Because it has an inventory it can use items if it has mana, to get mana you need to buy an item which gives mana and then use a regen pot. If you decide to give it mana, buy regen pot first and wait to buy the mana item until the second you want to do your play as builders will see it has 0/200 mana and be more attentive.
    - Useful items to use on mound:
        - Ethereal mirror, makes builders unable to gold.
        - Wotw, cyclones builders, combine with a tp and cyclone spell
        - Nuke rod
        - Voodoo doll, you can drop it in some spots and use it to jump spots which are unjumpable with items, [this for example](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/nu7GdxnOwJs). There is another spot in the north east entrance to mound.
        - Wand of neutralization
        - Wards
        - Shadowshard, stormrider, mistlords
    

Gold mound has a status bar, the teleport ability will show as a status effect, fake teleport and teleport staff will not.
