# Worker Stacking

Workers returning lumber are temporarily collisionless. As a consequence, if you spawn a worker in the window they are collisionless it will spawn ontop of the collisionless worker. Repeating this process you can stack all your workers in a tight, and preferably unnukeable area. However this process is time consuming and highly impractical, and even if you take the time to set it up fully workers sometimes get confused and refuse to return their lumber. 

It's good to know about it, but I don't think anyone trying to play optimally should be doing this.

# Nature Worker Stacking

Nature workers are temporarily collisionless when they spawn in. As a consequence if you don't set a rally point on your shelter they will all stack ontop of each other. This can be used to stack spears or mystics inside your towering area of the base. Although if titan has one or more minions they will be easily nuked out before sieging and useless.
