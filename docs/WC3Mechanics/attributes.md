# Attributes

As with the attack and defense types, island defense employs a custom attribute and leveling up system, to avoid confusion it's been renamed and reskinned to Vitality (str), Celerity (agi), and Wisdom (int). 

Each point of
    
- Vitality gives 50 health and 0.1 health regen per second.
- Celerity gives 0.25 movementspeed and 1.2% attack speed bonus.
- Wisdom gives 20 mana and 0.1 mana regen per second.

Each level up the titanous units gain a static +10 damage and 1.5 armor per level. Note that your primary attribute doesn't give you any damage as is the case for default WC3, the tooltip says it gives you damage but it doesn't.
