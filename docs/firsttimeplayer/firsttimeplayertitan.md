### Returning titan players

If you know the basics already from previous versions, here's what you need to know. 

- You scale very poorly off of levels compared to old versions, this means every item build needs to include damage items or we will spend 10 seconds to kill a wall entire game.
- You don't need to manually buy each items component and then recipe, if you have enough gold for the entire item the game will automatically buy it for you.
- Attributes and damage/defense types are reskinned, but they essentially change nothing compared to the old skins.
- Your mana regen is good, you don't need to buy mana coral or mana items. 

### First time titan player

Principles you should follow:

- Never show builders when you enter or exit invis unless it will yield a builder death.
- Always be productive with your time, and farm something.
- Don't worry about giving builders too much gold if it's your first game
    - it's really bad to give them too much gold but familiarize yourself with your skills, items before worrying about this.
- Constantly buy items you are aiming to buy, don't sit with 500g in your inventory.

Get these items in this order, buy subcomponents as you get the gold for it:

- Berserker's gem
- Berserker's gem
- Titanic trident
- stormrider's cloak
- pearl of grand vision
- armor of tides, give minion your ankh to make space for it
- dawnbreaker, give minion your pearl to make space for it
