Basic premise of Island Defense

You control a builder of size 1x1 and need to use buildings and units to, initially, avoid dying to the enemy titan of size 2x2 and eventually build a base. If your builder dies, you lose and spawn as a titanious minion which helps the titan.

You spawn inside the titans camp, located in the center of the map. Your inital goal is to gather lumber. Head towards an enclosed area at the edges of the map, preferably an area with 2 exits so you can leave through an open side if the titan attacks you, if you check the map in the terminology section LB1, LB2, LB3 are good options. Here you will build 1 shelter and 6 fruit bushes for 150 food supply, recruit harvesters from the shelter until your food supply is at 150/150 and order them to harvest lumber. Proceed to wall off your area such that no 2x2 unit can pass close to you or your harvesters, leave 1x1 gaps for your builder to freely move through. At 150 food supply you no longer need the fruit trees and can destroy them, all of your units and buildings feed the titan gold and experience, fruit trees and harvesters feed a lot while walls feed less. After your fruits are gone, you should have 150/6 food supply, 1 shelter, and a walled off protected area, at this point it's time to build your research center, when it completes upgrade your walls to the next tier.

Note that your harvesters have an ability to detonate themselves preventing them from massively feeding the titan in case you were to be attacked. Research increased lumber harvest acquisition rate from your shelter and wall health upgrade from your research center.

Next step is gathering gold, however it is risky, as it is done at the center of the map where you spawned in. Your builder is very fragile and it will die in roughly two melee attacks from the titan. Every builder has unique escape abilities to avoid dying, use them proactively before you are about to get attacked or you won't be able to react before you die. Read what your escape abilities do and then walk to mid, but never walk out in the open outside the cover of walls if the titan is hiding in the fog of war as the titan can be invisible and you basically instantly die if titan is allowed to melee attack you out of invisible.

Only gold when others are golding and always have your camera watching so you are able to react quickly. Once a builder dies and titan gets a minion, it is no longer safe to be around the middle area and it's time to base. Ask someone if they would want to pair up and base together with you. Bases are single entranced areas, and consist of 5 layers of walls and to start with around 30 towers but as the game progresses you want to fill your base upwards of around 60 towers. If you were able to reach 45g before a minion, that's great, then you can progress your towers to their final tier. If you weren't that might be okay, as you get 90g after the gold penalty timer runs out at the 30 minute mark.

TL;DR

  • Maximize your lumber
  • block off the area you are lumbering so no 2x2 unit can enter, detonate your workers if you are attacked
  • try to get gold in the middle of the map, you will die in 1-2 attacks from invis
  • dying means you lose the game
  • Once you have 45g or titan has a minion you need to find another builder to build a base with
