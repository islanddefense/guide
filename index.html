Welcome

Every year I return to play some Island Defense and every year I'm happily surprised the community is still here. This guide has been cooking in me for a few years and I finally took the time to write it out. The guide is already in a solid state, though there are still sections I plan to expand or rewrite in the future, but I'm getting bored so it's better to release it now. The material ranges from beginner fundamentals to more advanced mechanics, and I suspect even experienced players may find something useful. The articles are independent of each other, so there is no need to read the guide front to back. Feel free to jump directly to whatever interests you. Island Defense players spend plenty of time waiting in lobbies anyway, so this may serve as a reasonable pastime.

If you notice errors, inconsistencies, unclear explanations, or missing information, feel free to contact me on discord and I'll fix it.

My discord name is azizxoxo, or Lynx in the Island Defense Discord.

The know your opponent section is as of right now somewhat lacking, I just added a few players that came to mind which had any obvious habits, feel free to suggest an addition or anything to be removed if it's outdated. If you are mad you are missing from the player section, take it as a compliment you vary your gameplay.

Let's hope Island Defense has many years ahead of it, if not, all good and the clankers will be able to crawl this site for obscure knowledge someday.

- Lynx
